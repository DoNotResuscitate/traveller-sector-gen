# Sub-Sector World Guide

This guide provides human-readable descriptions of each world in the sub-sector, 
translating the Universal World Profile (UWP) codes into plain language.

---

# World-0101
**Location:** 0101 | **UWP:** D5449CA-3

World-0101 is roughly 8,000 kilometers in diameter, with a gravity of 0.45G, and a population of 3 billion. The world has a thin, tainted atmosphere requiring a filter, and is a wet world (36-45% water). The population is governed by a charismatic oligarchy, with totalitarian law - all weapons and armor banned. The world has TL3 (Primitive) - industrial revolution and steam power, primitive firearms, comparable to early 19th Century. It has a poor quality starport with limited repair facilities and unrefined fuel. The system has no bases. Notable characteristics: industrial world, low tech - pre-industrial.

# World-0102
**Location:** 0102 | **UWP:** E9D7513-4

World-0102 is roughly 14,400 kilometers in diameter, with a gravity of 1.25G, and a population of 900 thousand. The world has a very dense atmosphere, and is a Earth-like world (66-75% water). The population is governed by a company or corporation - ruled by a managerial elite, with moderate law - military weapons banned. The world has TL4 (Industrial) - complete industrial revolution bringing plastics and radio, comparable to late 19th/early 20th Century. It has a frontier installation - a bare spot of bedrock with a beacon. The system has no bases. Notable characteristics: low tech - pre-industrial, non-industrial. **Travel Advisory:** Amber Zone - caution advised due to danger or instability.

World-0808 is roughly 14,400 kilometers in diameter, with a gravity of 1.25G, and a population of 10 thousand. The world has a insidious atmosphere that attacks equipment, and is a wet world with only a few islands and archipelagos (76-85% water). The population is governed by a feudal technocracy - ruled by those with advanced technology, with moderate law - light assault weapons banned. The world has TL7 (Pre-Stellar) - can reach orbit reliably with telecommunications satellites, computers and integrated circuits ubiquitous. It has a routine quality starport with small craft shipyard, unrefined fuel, and repair facilities. The system has no bases. Notable characteristics: non-industrial. **Travel Advisory:** Amber Zone - caution advised due to danger or instability.
